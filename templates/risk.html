Risk Management Rules

1-2% Rule

Never risk more than 1-2% of total capital on a single trade. This ensures survival through losing streaks.

Risk/Reward Ratio

Aim for at least 1:2 risk/reward ratio. If you risk $100, target at least $200 profit. Minimum: 1:1.5

Stop Loss Always

Always set a stop loss before entering a trade. Never move it against you. The stop is sacred.

Portfolio Diversification

Limit total portfolio risk exposure. Don't have more than 6 correlated positions open simultaneously.
